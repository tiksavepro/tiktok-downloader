Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

At TikSave Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • TikTok URLs: The video links you paste to download videos (not stored permanently)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about site visits and downloads (no personal identification)
  • Browser Information: Device type, browser type, IP address (for security and optimization)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing your video download requests
  • Improving our service and user experience
  • Analyzing site performance and traffic patterns
  • Preventing abuse and ensuring security

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • No Account Required: We don't collect or store personal information like names, emails, or passwords
  • Temporary Processing: Video URLs are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your data with third parties

4. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies for:

  • Language preference storage
  • Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics or similar)
  • Site functionality improvements

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service uses:

  • RapidAPI: For fetching TikTok video data (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Netlify: For hosting (subject to their privacy policy)
  • TikTok CDN: For serving video files (we don't control TikTok's privacy practices)

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we may have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@tiksavepro.com (or your actual email)

10. Disclaimer

TikSave Pro is not affiliated with TikTok or ByteDance Ltd. All content downloaded belongs to their respective creators. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to download and use any content.
